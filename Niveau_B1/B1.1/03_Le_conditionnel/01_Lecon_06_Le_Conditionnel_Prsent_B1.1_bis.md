## Observation 1: When to use it?

**Look at the dialogue:**

1.  **Politeness / Softening a request:**
    *   "Tu **voudrais** venir ?" (Instead of "Tu veux venir ?")
2.  **Advice / Suggestion:**
    *   "Tu **devrais** prendre des vacances." (You should take...)
    *   "Tu **pourrais** louer une maison." (You could rent...)
3.  **Wish / Desire:**
    *   "J'**aimerais** partir." (I would like to leave.)
4.  **Hypothesis / Imaginary situation:**
    *   "ce **serait** fantastique." (It would be fantastic.)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid âŒ

1.  **Don't confuse Future and Conditional:**
    *   Future: Je parler**ai** (I will speak) -> Ending **-ai**
    *   Conditional: Je parler**ais** (I would speak) -> Ending **-ais**
    *   *Pronunciation hint:* In standard French, they sound slightly different (Ã© vs Ã¨), but often sound similar in rapid speech. Context is key!

2.  **Don't forget the 'r' in the stem:**
    *   Wrong: Je **fini**ais
    *   Right: Je **finir**ais (Infinitive + ending)
