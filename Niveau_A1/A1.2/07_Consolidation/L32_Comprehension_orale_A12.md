## Oral Interaction Scenarios

### Scenario 1: At a Restaurant
**Role-play with a partner or practice alone:**
- Order food and drinks
- Ask about Belgian specialties
- Ask for the bill

### Scenario 2: At the Doctor's
- Describe your symptoms
- Answer the doctor's questions
- Go to the pharmacy

### Scenario 3: At the Commune
- Explain why you're there
- Provide your personal information
- Ask questions about documents needed

### Scenario 4: Plan an Outing
- Suggest an activity to a friend
- Discuss when and where to meet
- Handle a refusal and suggest an alternative

### Scenario 5: Apartment Visit
- Ask questions about the apartment
- Describe what you're looking for
- Negotiate (ask about charges, availability, etc.)

### Scenario 6: At the Market
- Ask for quantities (un kilo de, une barquette de...)
- Use partitive articles (du fromage, de la salade...)
- Ask the price and pay
- Use Belgian numbers (septante, nonante)

### Scenario 7: Cooking Together
- Give instructions to a partner using the imperative
- Name ingredients with partitive articles
- Tell the time for each step (pendant 10 minutes, Ã  midi...)
