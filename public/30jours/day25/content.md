### The "Paper" Culture
Belgian employers value credentials.
*   **Diplomas:** Ensure your foreign degree is recognized (Equivalence) if applying for government jobs.
*   **Certifications:** Adding a "Prince2" (Project Management) or language certificate to your CV is powerful.

### Networking Etiquette
*   **LinkedIn:** Very active in Belgium. Keep it updated.
*   **The Lunch Meeting:** The primary venue for business relationship building.
    *   *Rule:* Keep it personal for the first 15 mins. Don't jump straight to business.
    *   *Paying:* Usually the person who invited pays, or you split.
