### Embracing "Quartier" Loyalty
People in Brussels are very proud of their commune.
"I'm a Saint-Gillois." "I'm from Etterbeek."
Get to know your local shopkeepers.
Greeting the baker with a cheerful *"Bonjour !"* is mandatory.
Leaving without saying *"Merci, au revoir !"* is a crime.
These micro-interactions build your social web.
