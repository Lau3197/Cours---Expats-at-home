## �x� Belgian Listening Tips

**Common Belgian French sounds and expressions:**

1. **Numbers**: Listen for:
   - Septante (70), nonante (90)
   - Not soixante-dix or quatre-vingt-dix!

2. **"R" pronunciation**: 
   - Belgian R is often softer than in France
   - Sometimes rolled slightly

3. **Common Belgian expressions**:
   - "Tantôt" = later/in a bit (not "this afternoon" like in France)
   - "Une fois" = just a filler word (means nothing special)
   - "Chouette!" = Great!/Cool!
   - "S'il vous plaît" is ALWAYS used (very important!)

4. **Intonation**:
   - Belgians tend to speak more slowly than Parisians
   - Questions rise at the end
   - Politeness is emphasized with softer tone
