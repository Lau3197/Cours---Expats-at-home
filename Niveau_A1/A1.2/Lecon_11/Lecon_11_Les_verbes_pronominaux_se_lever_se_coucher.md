## Grammar: Reflexive Verbs

## What Are Reflexive Verbs?

**Reflexive verbs** are verbs where the subject does the action to themselves. In English, we often use "myself", "yourself", etc.

**Examples:**
- **Je me l√®ve** = "I get up" (I get myself up)
- **Tu te couches** = "You go to bed" (You put yourself to bed)
- **Il se douche** = "He takes a shower" (He showers himself)

## How to Conjugate Reflexive Verbs

**The reflexive pronouns:**
- **me** (myself) - before "je"
- **te** (yourself) - before "tu"
- **se** (himself/herself) - before "il/elle"
- **nous** (ourselves) - before "nous"
- **vous** (yourselves) - before "vous"
- **se** (themselves) - before "ils/elles"
