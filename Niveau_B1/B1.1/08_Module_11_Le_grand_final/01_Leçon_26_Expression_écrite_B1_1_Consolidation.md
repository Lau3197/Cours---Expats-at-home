# Leçon 29 : Expression écrite B1.1 (Consolidation)

**Niveau** : B1.1  
**Type** : Consolidation (Écriture)  
**Durée estimée** : 2-3 heures  
**Objectifs** : Practice B1 writing skills

---

## B1 Writing Skills

### What you should be able to write at B1

- Structured texts on familiar topics
- Formal and informal emails
- Narratives about experiences
- Opinion texts with arguments
- Short reports

---

## Practice 1: Email writing

**Write a formal email (100-120 words):**

**Situation:** You want to enroll in a French course. Write to the school to:
- Ask about schedules and prices
- Explain your current level
- Ask about placement tests

**Structure:**
- Opening (Madame, Monsieur,)
- Purpose
- Questions
- Closing

---

## Practice 2: Narrative writing

**Write about a past experience (120-150 words):**

**Topic:** "Un voyage mémorable" or "Une expérience difficile"

**Include:**
- Setting (imparfait)
- Main events (passé composé)
- Earlier context (plus-que-parfait)
- Your feelings

**Useful structures:**
- Quand je suis arrivé(e)...
- J'avais déjà...
- C'était... parce que...
- Finalement,...

---

## Practice 3: Opinion writing

**Write your opinion (100-120 words):**

**Topic:** "Le télétravail est-il une bonne chose ?"

**Structure:**
- Introduction: state the topic
- Arguments for (D'une part, Premièrement...)
- Arguments against (D'autre part, Cependant...)
- Your opinion (À mon avis, Je pense que...)
- Conclusion

**Useful connectors:**
- D'abord / Ensuite / Enfin
- D'une part... d'autre part
- Bien que + subjunctive
- Par conséquent

---

## Practice 4: Short report

**Write a brief report (100-120 words):**

**Topic:** Report on a project or event (real or imagined)

**Include:**
- What was done (passive voice)
- Results
- Next steps

**Useful structures:**
- Le projet a été lancé...
- Les objectifs ont été atteints...
- Il a été décidé que...
- Les prochaines étapes seront...

---

## Writing Checklist

### Content
- [ ] I answered the question completely
- [ ] Ideas are logical and organized
- [ ] I included all required elements

### Structure
- [ ] Clear introduction/body/conclusion
- [ ] Good paragraphing
- [ ] Appropriate connectors

### Language
- [ ] Correct verb tenses
- [ ] Appropriate vocabulary
- [ ] Gender/number agreement
- [ ] Correct subjunctive use

### Style
- [ ] Appropriate register (formal/informal)
- [ ] Varied sentence structures
- [ ] No repetition
