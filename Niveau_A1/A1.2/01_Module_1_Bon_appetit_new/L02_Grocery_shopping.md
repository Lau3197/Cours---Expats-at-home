## Observation 1: Partitive Articles — DU / DE LA / DE L' / DES

**When talking about an unspecified quantity (some/any), use partitive articles:**

| Gender | Article | Example |
|:-------|:--------|:--------|
| Masculine | **du** | **du** pain (some bread) |
| Feminine | **de la** | **de la** confiture (some jam) |
| Before vowel | **de l'** | **de l'**eau (some water) |
| Plural | **des** | **des** œufs (some eggs) |

**How is this different from LE/LA/LES?**

| Article | Meaning | Example |
|:--------|:--------|:--------|
| **Le/La/Les** | THE (specific or general) | J'aime **le** chocolat. (I like chocolate in general) |
| **Du/De la/Des** | SOME (unspecified quantity) | Je mange **du** chocolat. (I eat some chocolate) |

### Practice 1: Drill — Choose the Right Partitive

1. Je mange _________ pain. (bread — m)
2. On boit _________ eau. (water — before vowel)
3. Elle achète _________ fromage. (cheese — m)
4. Il y a _________ confiture. (jam — f)
5. On mange _________ pâtes. (pasta — plural)

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. **du** — 2. **de l'** — 3. **du** — 4. **de la** — 5. **des**

</details>

## Observation 2: "PAS DE" in Negation

**In the negative, du/de la/de l'/des ALL become DE:**

| Positive | Negative |
|:---------|:---------|
| J'ai **du** lait. | Je n'ai pas **de** lait. |
| Il y a **de la** viande. | Il n'y a pas **de** viande. |
| On a **des** œufs. | On n'a pas **d'**œufs. |

**The rule:** After NE...PAS → du/de la/des → **DE** (or **d'** before a vowel)

---

### Practice 2: Drill — Make It Negative

1. J'ai du pain. → ___________
2. On a de la confiture. → ___________
3. Il y a des tomates. → ___________
4. Elle boit de l'eau. → ___________

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. Je **n'ai pas de** pain.
2. On **n'a pas de** confiture.
3. Il **n'y a pas de** tomates.
4. Elle **ne boit pas d'**eau.

</details>

## Culture: Shopping in Belgium

### Belgian Supermarkets
- **Delhaize** — Moderate prices, good quality
- **Colruyt** — Cheapest guaranteed
- **Carrefour** — French chain, everywhere
- **Aldi / Lidl** — Budget-friendly
- **Bio Planet** — Organic

### Useful Shopping Expressions

| French | English |
|:-------|:--------|
| Il me faut... | I need... |
| C'est combien ? | How much is it? |
| Où est le rayon... ? | Where is the... section? |
| Vous avez un sac ? | Do you have a bag? |
