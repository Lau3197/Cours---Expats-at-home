Day 14. Two weeks down.
You are no longer a "new arrival." You are a resident in training.
You have a mutuelle. You know where to buy groceries without going bankrupt. You have made a friend (or at least talked to a stranger).

Connecting with people, connecting with colleagues, connecting with the city.
Integration is a marathon, not a sprint.

That's normal. That's love.
