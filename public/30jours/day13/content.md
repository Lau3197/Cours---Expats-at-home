### Secret Parks
*   **Parc d'Egmont:** Hidden behind the Hilton hotel off Avenue Louise. A secret garden with statues and a cafe (La Fabrique).
*   **Jardins du Fleuriste (Laeken):** Panoramic views of the city, totally off the radar.
*   **Abbey of La Cambre:** Stunning Cistercian abbey with landscaped gardens.
