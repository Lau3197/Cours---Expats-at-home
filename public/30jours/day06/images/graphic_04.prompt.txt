**TITLE**: At the Shop or Restaurant
**VISUAL STRUCTURE**: A clear, distinct list of phrase cards or an icon-driven grid.
**CONTENT**:
Each entry should prominently display the French phrase with its English translation.
1.  **Je voudrais…**
    *   (I would like…)
    *   *Note*: More polite than "Je veux" (I want).
    *   *Example*: "Je voudrais un café, s'il vous plaît."
2.  **C'est combien?**
    *   (How much is it?)
3.  **L'addition, s'il vous plaît.**
    *   (The bill, please.)
4.  **Par carte? / En espèces?**
    *   (By card? / In cash?)
