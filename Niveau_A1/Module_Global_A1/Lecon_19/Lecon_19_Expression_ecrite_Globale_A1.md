## ðŸŽ¯ DELF A1 Writing Strategy

### Time Management (30 minutes total):

1. **Read the task carefully** (2 min)
   - Underline key information
   - Note what you need to include

2. **Plan your message** (3 min)
   - Write key words in your mind
   - Check the format required

3. **Write your text** (15 min)
   - Write clearly and legibly
   - Count words as you go
   - Leave space for corrections

4. **Check your work** (10 min)
   - Read your text 2-3 times
   - Check against the checklist
   - Make corrections neatly
