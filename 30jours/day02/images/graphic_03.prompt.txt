**Visual Structure**: Checklist graphic.

**Content**:
**Header**: Documents You Need
**Main Item 1**: Proof of Identity
    *   Bullet 1: Passport
    *   Bullet 2: National ID Card (from home country)
    *   *Associated Icon*: An ID card or passport.
**Main Item 2**: Proof of Address
    *   Bullet 1: Rental Contract
    *   Bullet 2: Recent Utility Bill
    *   Bullet 3: Official Registration Document (Town Hall / *gemeentehuis* / *maison communale*)
    *   *Associated Icon*: A house or document with an address.
**Callout Box / Side Note**: NISS Number
    *   Text: Belgian Social Security Number. Needed for tax purposes, but you can often open the account before you have it (let them know it's coming).
