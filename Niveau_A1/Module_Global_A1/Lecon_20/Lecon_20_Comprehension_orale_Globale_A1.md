## ðŸŽ¯ DELF A1 Listening Strategy

### Before Listening:

1. **Read the questions first** (you have time before the audio)
   - Underline key words
   - Predict what information you need to listen for
   - Note if questions ask for specific numbers, names, times, places

2. **Prepare to take notes**
   - Have a pen ready
   - Prepare to write key information

### During First Listening:

1. **Focus on general understanding**
   - Who is speaking?
   - What is the situation?
   - What is the main topic?

2. **Note key information**
   - Numbers, times, prices
   - Names and places
   - Key words you recognize

3. **Don't panic if you miss something**
   - You will hear it again!
   - Focus on what you DO understand

### During Second Listening:

1. **Fill in the gaps**
   - Listen specifically for information you missed
   - Check your answers
   - Verify numbers and spelling

2. **Answer remaining questions**
   - Use the questions as a guide
   - Listen in order (questions usually follow the audio order)

### After Listening:

1. **Answer all questions**
   - Never leave a blank!
   - If you're not sure, make an educated guess
   - Use logic and context

2. **Check your answers**
   - Do they make sense?
   - Did you answer in the correct format?
   - Check spelling of written answers
