### Listen to this dialogue

## Observation 1: The Verb FAIRE (to do / to make)

**Look at the dialogue:**

> tu **fais** — je **fais** — je **fais** — il **fait** — elle **fait** — ils **font**

**FAIRE is an irregular verb**, but it's one of the most used verbs in French!

### Full Conjugation of FAIRE

| Subject | Conjugation | Pronunciation |
|:--------|:------------|:-------------|
| je | **fais** | /ʒə fɛ/ |
| tu | **fais** | /ty fɛ/ |
| il/elle/on | **fait** | /il fɛ/ |
| nous | **faisons** | /nu fə.zɔ̃/ |
| vous | **faites** | /vu fɛt/ |
| ils/elles | **font** | /il fɔ̃/ |

**Note:** "Nous faisons" has a special pronunciation: [fuh-ZON], not [fay-ZON].

### Asking "What Do You Do?"

| French | English | Register |
|:-------|:--------|:---------|
| **Qu'est-ce que** tu fais ? | What are you doing? | Standard |
| Tu fais **quoi** ? | What are you doing? | Informal |
| **Qu'est-ce que** vous faites ? | What are you doing? | Formal |

**"Qu'est-ce que" is one of the most useful question forms in French!** You already saw it in Lesson 4 for professions.

## Observation 2: Contracted Articles with DE — DU, DE LA, DE L', DES

**Look at the expressions with FAIRE:**

> faire **du** sport (du = de + le)
> faire **de la** natation
> faire **de la** cuisine
> faire **des** pâtes (des = de + les)

**This is the same contraction system as with À, but with DE!**

### The Rules

| de + article | Result | Example |
|:------------|:-------|:--------|
| de + **le** | → **du** | faire **du** sport |
| de + **la** | → **de la** | faire **de la** cuisine |
| de + **l'** | → **de l'** | faire **de l'**exercice |
| de + **les** | → **des** | faire **des** courses |

**Only "de + le" and "de + les" contract!** "De la" and "de l'" stay the same.

### Common Expressions with FAIRE + DU/DE LA

| French | English |
|:-------|:--------|
| faire **du** sport | to do sports |
| faire **du** vélo | to cycle |
| faire **du** yoga | to do yoga |
| faire **de la** natation | to swim |
| faire **de la** cuisine | to cook |
| faire **de la** musique | to make music |
| faire **de l'**exercice | to exercise |
| faire **des** courses | to do grocery shopping |
| faire **des** promenades | to go for walks |

## Culture: Sports and Activities in Belgium

### Popular Activities

| Activity | French | Note |
|:---------|:-------|:-----|
| Cycling | faire **du** vélo | Belgium = cycling country! Tour of Flanders, Liège-Bastogne-Liège |

**Carlos, Brazilian in Brussels:**

> "What I love about Belgium is that we cycle a lot. There are bike paths everywhere in Brussels. And when the weather is nice, we go for walks in the Sonian Forest!"

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Saying "de le" instead of "du"

| Wrong | Correct |
|:------|:--------|
| Je fais **de le** sport. | Je fais **du** sport. |
| Je fais **de les** courses. | Je fais **des** courses. |

**de + le = du** and **de + les = des** — always!

### Mistake 2: Confusing AU and DU

| AU (à + le) = "to the" | DU (de + le) = "of the / some" |
|:------------------------|:-------------------------------|
| Je vais **au** parc. (I go to the park) | Je fais **du** sport. (I do sports) |
| On va **au** marché. (We go to the market) | On fait **du** vélo. (We cycle) |

**AU = direction (going to). DU = activity/quantity (doing/some).**

### Mistake 3: Forgetting expressions with FAIRE

| Wrong | Correct |
|:------|:--------|
| Je **fais** les sports. | Je **fais du** sport. |
| Je **fais** la natation. | Je **fais de la** natation. |

For activities with faire → du/de la/de l'.

## Grammar Summary

### FAIRE (to do/make)

| Subject | Conjugation |
|:--------|:------------|
| je | fais |
| tu | fais |
| il/elle/on | fait |
| nous | faisons |
| vous | faites |
| ils/elles | font |

### Contracted Articles with DE

| Combination | Result | Example |
|:-----------|:-------|:--------|
| de + le | **du** | faire du sport |
| de + la | de la | faire de la cuisine |
| de + l' | de l' | faire de l'exercice |
| de + les | **des** | faire des courses |

### AU vs DU — Summary

| With À (direction) | With DE (activity/origin) |
|:-------------------|:------------------------|
| **au** (à + le) | **du** (de + le) |
| **à la** | **de la** |
| **à l'** | **de l'** |
| **aux** (à + les) | **des** (de + les) |
