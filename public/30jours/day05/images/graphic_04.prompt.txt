Visual Structure: Two-column comparative checklist.

**Title:** Exchanging Your Driving License in Belgium

**Column 1: Non-EU Country License**
*   **Action:** Must exchange for a Belgian license.
*   **Deadline:** Within 185 days (~6 months) of registering address in Belgium.

**Column 2: EU Country License**
*   **Flexibility:** Can continue to use until it expires.
*   **When Exchanging (at expiration or voluntarily):**
    *   **Where:** Local Town Hall (*commune* or *maison communale*)
    *   **Required Documents:**
        *   Original License
        *   ID or Residence Permit
        *   Passport Photo
    *   **Cost:** ~25-30 euros
