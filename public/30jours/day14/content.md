### The "Frituur" Ritual üçü
You cannot finish Week 2 without a proper Fritkot experience.
**How to order like a local:**
1.  **The Fries:** "Un paquet de frites" / "Une grande frite."
2.  **The Sauce:** Mayo is classic. Andalouse (spicy tomato/mayo) is the local favorite. Samurai is hot.
3.  **The Snack:** Fricadelle (don't ask what's in it), Croquette de fromage (cheese), Poulcycroc.
4.  **The Drink:** Jupiler or Maes (Pilsner beer).
