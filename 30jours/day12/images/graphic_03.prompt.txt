**VISUAL STRUCTURE:** A split-screen or two-column layout clearly separating 'Accepting' and 'Declining'.

**CONTENT:**

**Left Side: Accepting Invitations**
*   **Header:** To Accept (or Accepting Invitations)
*   **Key Idea:** Be warm and direct.
*   **Phrases:**
    *   **Oui, avec plaisir!** (Yes, with pleasure!)
    *   **Volontiers, merci.** (Gladly, thank you.)
    *   **C'est très gentil, merci pour l'invitation.** (That's very kind, thank you for the invitation.)

**Right Side: Declining Invitations**
*   **Header:** To Decline (or Declining Invitations)
*   **Key Idea:** Be polite, clear, and brief. No long excuses needed.
*   **Phrases:**
    *   **Je suis désolé(e), mais je ne serai pas disponible ce jour-là.** (I'm sorry, but I won't be available that day.)
    *   **Merci beaucoup pour l'invitation, mais j'ai déjà quelque chose de prévu.** (Thanks so much for the invitation, but I already have something planned.)
    *   **Dommage, une autre fois peut-être?** (That's a shame, another time maybe?)
*   **Note for last phrase:** Shows interest, even if unavailable.
