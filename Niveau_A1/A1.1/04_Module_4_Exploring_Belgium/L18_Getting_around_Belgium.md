## Let's Start! Lost in Brussels

### Listen to this dialogue

**Tom is trying to get to the European Quarter. He asks a passerby:**

> **Tom :** Excusez-moi, comment est-ce que je vais au quartier européen ?
>
> **Passant :** C'est facile ! Vous **prenez** le métro. La station est là-bas, à gauche.
>
> **Tom :** Je prends quelle ligne ?
>
> **Passant :** Vous **prenez** la ligne 1, direction Stockel. Vous descendez à la station Schuman.
>
> **Tom :** C'est loin d'ici ?
>
> **Passant :** Non, c'est à cinq minutes en métro. Vous pouvez aussi **prendre** le tram 81, l'arrêt est en face.
>
> **Tom :** D'accord, merci beaucoup !
>
> **Passant :** Avec plaisir ! Bonne journée !

<details>
<summary>English Translation</summary>

> **Tom:** Excuse me, how do I get to the European Quarter?
>
> **Passerby:** It's easy! You **take** the metro. The station is over there, on the left.
>
> **Tom:** Which line do I take?
>
> **Passerby:** You **take** line 1, direction Stockel. You get off at Schuman station.
>
> **Tom:** Is it far from here?
>
> **Passerby:** No, it's five minutes by metro. You can also **take** tram 81, the stop is across the street.
>
> **Tom:** OK, thank you very much!
>
> **Passerby:** With pleasure! Have a nice day!

</details>

---

### Comprehension

1. Where does Tom want to go?
2. What transport does the person suggest?
3. What metro line should Tom take?
4. Where does he get off?
5. How long does it take?

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. The **European Quarter** — 2. The **metro** (or tram 81) — 3. **Line 1** — 4. **Schuman** station — 5. **5 minutes**

</details>

### Belgian Transport Companies

| Company | Area | Note |
|:--------|:-----|:-----|
| **STIB / MIVB** | Brussels | Metro, tram, bus in Brussels |

| **SNCB / NMBS** | All Belgium | National trains |

**The Mobib card** is your rechargeable transport card for Brussels (STIB).

## Observation 2: The Verb PRENDRE (to take) — Preview

**Look at the dialogue:**

> Vous **prenez** le métro.
> Vous **prenez** la ligne 1.
> Vous pouvez aussi **prendre** le tram.

**PRENDRE is an irregular verb**, very useful for transport!

### Conjugation of PRENDRE

| Subject | Conjugation | Pronunciation |
|:--------|:------------|:-------------|
| je | **prends** | [pron] |
| tu | **prends** | [pron] |
| il/elle/on | **prend** | [pron] |
| nous | **prenons** | [pruh-NON] |
| vous | **prenez** | [pruh-NAY] |
| ils/elles | **prennent** | [pren] |

### With Transport

| French | English |
|:-------|:--------|
| Je **prends** le bus. | I take the bus. |
| Tu **prends** le métro ? | Do you take the metro? |
| On **prend** le tram. | We take the tram. |

## Culture: Getting Around Belgium

### Brussels Transport Tips

- **Mobib card:** Buy it at any STIB station. Load a monthly pass (49€) or single trips (2,40€ per trip).
- **Metro:** 4 lines (1, 2, 5, 6). Runs from 5h30 to midnight.
- **Tram:** Very popular in Brussels! Some lines are pre-metro (underground).
- **Bus:** Covers areas where metro/tram doesn't go.
- **Vélo:** Villo! bike-sharing system (like Vélib' in Paris).

### Between Cities

| From Brussels to... | Transport | Duration |
|:-------------------|:----------|:---------|
